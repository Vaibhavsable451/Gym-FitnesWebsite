Best Fitness Tips

  • Stay consistent with workouts at least 4 to 5 days a week.
  • Prioritize protein, vegetables, fruits, and proper hydration.
  • Sleep 7 to 8 hours daily for muscle recovery and energy.
  • Warm up before exercise and stretch after training.
  • Track your progress with photos, weight, and strength improvements.
